About This Album — by LPH

This collection isn’t just a series of photos — it’s a place where I keep the little things that matter. Moments that felt warm, stories I didn’t want to forget, and frames that remind me of who I was at that exact second.

Think of it like a personal playlist — but instead of music, it’s made of memories. Each image is a track, each scene a feeling, and together they shape a small visual story of my life.

Some shots are spontaneous, some intentional, but all of them carry a piece of me: the way I see beauty, the way I hold onto things that pass too quickly, and the way I turn everyday life into something worth keeping.

This album is my archive of moments — simple, honest, and meaningful. A quiet place where LPH stores the things he doesn’t want to lose.
